Get Involved

Join Us!

The best way to get involved in WACM is to attend our events. We typically host a variety of events throughout the year, including visits to tech offices such as Epic and Google, attending the Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing, meals with distinguished women in the tech field, informational sessions on Linux, club brunches, social lunches, and mentoring events.

Mailing List

Join our (low volume) mailing list for announcements of upcoming events and so forth. The list is maintained by the CSL using the Mailman program. You can subscribe to the list by going to the subscription page. Please subscribe with your wisc.edu or cs.wisc.edu email address.

Mentoring Program

We pair CS grad student mentors and ungrad mentees to connect mentees with a person to consult for computer science information (e.g. opportunities in CS, various sub-fields of CS, industry vs. graduate school, etc) and help them build a network of peers in computer science. WACM mentoring events will help you meet other women in the CS department, learn about CS, and of course, have fun! For more information, contact the Mentoring Co-Chairs.

Grace Hopper Celebration Scholarship

WACM provides scholarships for women to attend the Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing. It is the world's largest gathering of women technologists and truly an incredible experience to learn more about the technology industry and network with other amazing women. Make sure to apply at the end of spring semester!
